# eClaims System Requirements

## 1. Overview
The eClaims Application is a single, integrated solution designed to modernize the claims processing part of YCompany's business.

## 2. Primary Portals (User-Facing)

### A. Customer Portal and Mobile App
*   **Users**: Customers.
*   **Features**:
    *   Submit claims.
    *   Upload supporting documents (photos, police reports).
    *   Track claim status.
    *   Check partner service providers.
    *   Select rental vehicles.
    *   Make electronic payments.

### B. 3rd Party Provider Portal
*   **Users**: Partners (Repair workshops, Car rental partners).
*   **Features**:
    *   Log in to upload work orders and estimates.
    *   Provide work status updates.
    *   Provide final bill amount.
    *   Track claims payment status.

### C. Internal Portal
*   **Users**: Case Managers, Incident Managers, Adjustors, Surveyors, Claims Dept staff, Regional Managers, Auditors.
*   **Features**:
    *   Submit assessments.
    *   Adjudicate claims.
    *   Delegate processing.
    *   Generate reports.

## 3. Supporting Sub-Systems/Modules

### A. Alert and Notification System
*   Analyzes time to resolve incidents.
*   Sends notifications (SMS/Email) to relevant parties on status changes.
*   Archives all customer communication.

### B. Document Management System (DMS)
*   Critical for auditing and compliance.
*   Centralizes storage and archiving of every claims document.

### C. Reporting Engine
*   Generates detailed reports for Claims department and management.
*   Metrics: Processing time, fraudulent claims, ageing matrix, geo-based statistics.

### D. Identity Management System
*   Role-based user authentication and authorization across all portals.


The front end will be Asp.net mvc , API and C# , mongo db
